Quick Guide

Create An Account

1. Input all the informations needed.

2. Check if your information is all correct, it cannot be updated unless assistance is contacted.

3. Join a specific class using your teacher's class code.

Register Now

Join a Class

1. Copy the class code given by your teacher.

2. Go to "Classes" page and click "Join Class".

3. Finally, paste the class code and welcome to your new class.

Pass Your Works

1. Go to "Classes" and look for the activity you want to pass.

2. Look for the "Submit" button and click it.

3. Lastly, upload your file and click finish for your teacher to receive it.

Enjoy While Learning With Points!

1. Login everyday to get daily points.

2. You can get points by passing activities earlier or before its deadline.
